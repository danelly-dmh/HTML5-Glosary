FigCaption

You can locate <figcaption> tag as the first or last child of its parent <figure> element. The <figcaption> tag comes in pairs. The content must be written between opening ( <figcaption> ) and closing ( </figcaption> ) tags. Only one <figcaption> tag may be nested within a <figure>, although the <figure> tag itself may contain multiple other child elements (e.g., <img> or <code>).
